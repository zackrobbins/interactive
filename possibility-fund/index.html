A Different Approach to Sustainable Development

We have all see funding, programs, and leaders come and go, having failed to realize their good intentions. Without changing the underlying beliefs, it is impossible to gain full traction and make change sustainable.

Sustainable change relies on changing mindsets rather than circumstances. Mindsets are the assumptions and beliefs that shape behaviors and results. It is almost impossible to see mindsets, much less change them. But, when mindsets can be illuminated and changed, circumstances look different and new solutions emerge. Unexpected and dramatic results follow.

Mindsets can change. And when mindsets change, everything changes.

Change in Chester, PA

Transforming Chester starts with transforming the education system. Change in the Chester Upland School District (CUSD) means:

  • Every student and every adult feels cared for, valued, and supported, every hour of every day.
  • Every student graduates high school ready for college or a career.

Everyone can learn and learn at a high level. We have the ability to make the difference.
